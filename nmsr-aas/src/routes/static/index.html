NickAc's Minecraft Skin Renderer

A service written in Rust to render Minecraft skins with true perspective.
This works by using pre-rendered UV maps that are then merged together into the output image.

API endpoints

GET /fullbody/{uuid|texture hash} - Render a player's full body

Render a player's Minecraft skin onto a PNG image.

You can specify either a valid Minecraft account UUID or a texture hash.

You can also specify the ?alex query parameter to use the Alex model.

Examples:

  • Full body render of NickAc's current skin
  • Full body render of Koide's current skin
  • Full body render of BmaxXSux's current skin
  • Full body render of an old skin of BmaxXSux
  • Full body render of an old skin of Koide

GET /skin/{uuid|texture hash} - Fetch a player's skin

Get the player's Minecraft skin returned as a PNG image.

You can specify either a valid Minecraft account UUID or a texture hash.

Examples:

  • Skin of NickAc
  • Skin of Koide
  • Skin of BmaxXSux
  • An old skin of BmaxXSux
  • An old skin of Koide
